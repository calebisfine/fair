Discussions are Unfair

Straight, white, cisgender men speak louder, sooner, and more often than their peers of other genders and identities.

Other Examples

  • Racing to answer a question first without giving others an opportunity
  • Receiving praise for an answer or observation that someone else already shared
  • Offering advice or opinions without being asked
  • Assuming someone’s level of knowledge or experience without a good reason
  • Avoiding talking to someone because you can’t pronounce their name or understand their accent
